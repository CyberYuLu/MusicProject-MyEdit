History
I created Clusters in 2016 and made it available on ventrella.com. The following year I worked with the guys at Leap Motion on implementing Clusters in 3D for virtual reality, using hand/finger controls.

Clusters was the inspiration for Particle Life by Tom Mohr. Clusters was also the inspiration for Atomic Clusters by ciphrd.
